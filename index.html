What we do

Show, not tell that you know the subject matter

The ISTO standardized test is an evidence-based test to measure an individual’s level of understanding within the syllabus of a defined subject matter, such as the ISO 9001:2015 management system standard.

  • Get certificated for your time spent training

  • Prove that you are knowledgeable with a simple multiple choice test 

  • No prerequisite requirements

  • Take the test from the comfort of your home

More about us

A certificate, data points and beyond

What do you receive from an ISTO Test

Certificate of Achievement

Qualification: a certificate of achievement upon passing and certificates your  understanding of the respective standard. 

Analytics Report

Analysis: an analytics report which measures your level of understanding in the eight (8) A C C U R A T E domains

Ranking 

Star Diagram Explanation: ranks a candidate’s performance against the candidate population
